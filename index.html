    Style: Art, Fiction

    Jenny Martin is the author of the young adult sci-fi novels Tracked and Marked, known for their action-packed blend of racing, rebellion, and futuristic intrigue. Her debut, Tracked, follows Phee Van Zant, a skilled street racer entangled in political conspiracies and resistance against corporate control. The novel has been praised for its engaging world-building and fast-paced narrative, often compared to The Fast and the Furious meets Firefly for its thrilling mix of high-stakes racing and adventure. Martin’s work is celebrated for its strong, relatable protagonists and has been well received by both YA readers and sci-fi enthusiasts​.
